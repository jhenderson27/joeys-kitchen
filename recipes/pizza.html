Infinity Pizza


Description

A good infinity pizza isn't really about the ingredients at all, we're going for quantity over quality here. Whatever you can scrape together for toppings will work, I just happened to have some locally sourced organic free range low sodium gluten free pepperoni on hand when writing this recipe up so that's what I used.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Red sauce
  • Toppings!

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450Â°
  2. Toss or roll the dough out into a circle
  3. Spread the sauce (doesn't really matter how close you get to the edge here since we're going for an infinity pizza there won't actually be an edge when we're done)
  4. Add the cheese
  5. Add the toppings
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust starts to crisp
  7. Hit the infinitizer switch

There you have it!
